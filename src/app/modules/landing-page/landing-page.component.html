What is The Coffee Lab?

The Coffee Lab is a platform for anyone in the coffee world, from roasters to estates to home brewers, to share their views, expertise, and experiences. Members can ask questions, provide answers, talk about their brands, share tips, write about coffee culture, and much more. If you love coffee and want to share that, this is the place for you!

More about writing on The Coffee Lab

Visit eraofwe.com and click 'Register Now'. You can now create a profile with your name, bio, location, etc. Once registered, simply visit The Coffee Lab to get started!

Anyone who works with coffee or loves coffee! We're an inclusive platform that caters to everyone in the coffee world.

You can post articles, proofread, translate, share recipes or brewing guides, and ask or answer questions. Of course, you can also simply browse the articles, recipes, and Q&As and interact with others on the platform.

Roasters can share their brand story directly with customers and build trust with their audience by sharing their expertise and views. The Coffee Lab enables roasters to reach out to a new niche of customers and create their own brand community.

Estates on The Coffee Lab have the opportunity to interact with consumers first-hand to promote trust and transparency. They can build their brand value directly with a global audience and tell the story behind their coffee.

Cafes, restaurants, and baristas can use The Coffee Lab to position themselves as trusted voices in the industry, sharing their expertise and experience with consumers. They can also build long-term relationships with roasters and estates to diversify their business and offerings.

The Coffee Lab is a great place for coffee lovers to gather and discuss their favorite coffee while also discovering new, sustainable coffee brands that are changing the future of coffee.
