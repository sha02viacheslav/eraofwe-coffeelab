What is The Coffee Lab?

The Coffee Lab is a platform for anyone in the coffee world, from roasters to estates to home brewers, to share their views, expertise, and experiences. Members can ask questions, provide answers, talk about their brands, share tips, write about coffee culture, and much more. If you love coffee and want to share that, this is the place for you!
